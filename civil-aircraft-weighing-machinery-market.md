<p><strong>The Civil Aircraft Weighing Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Platform System</li><li>Jack Weigh System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Civil Aircraft Weighing Machinery market comprises two types of systems - Platform System and Jack Weigh System. The Platform System involves the use of a weighing platform, where the aircraft is placed on top for weighing. This system is suitable for smaller aircraft and provides accurate weight measurements. On the other hand, the Jack Weigh System utilizes hydraulic jacks to lift the aircraft off the ground, allowing for individual weighing of different landing gears. This system is ideal for larger aircraft and provides precise weight distribution analysis. Both systems are essential for ensuring safety and efficiency in aviation operations.</p></p>
