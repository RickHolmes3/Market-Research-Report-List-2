<p><p>The automatic vehicle washing system market can be categorized into three types. Tunnel type automatic vehicle washing systems are designed to wash vehicles while they pass through a tunnel-like structure with automated brushes and water jets. Rollovers type automatic vehicle washing systems involve a stationary vehicle and a mechanical arm with brushes that move over the vehicle to clean it. Touchless in-bay automatic vehicle washing systems use high-pressure water jets and chemicals to clean the vehicle without any physical contact.</p></p>
