<p><ul><li>HDI PCB (1+N+1)</li><li>HDI PCB (2+N+2)</li><li>ELIC (Every Layer Interconnection)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HDI PCB refers to High-Density Interconnect Printed Circuit Boards, which are designed to accommodate more complex circuitry in a smaller space. There are different market types within the HDI PCB category. These include 1+N+1, where 1 represents a single high-density layer and N represents normal layers; 2+N+2, which adds an additional high-density layer on both sides; and ELIC, which allows interconnections on every layer. These market types cater to specific needs and requirements for compact and intricate circuit designs, offering increased functionality and efficiency.</p></p>
