<p><strong>The Oil-Free Diaphragm Vacuum Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Claw Vacuum Pumps</li><li>Screw Vacuum Pumps</li><li>Scroll Vacuum Pumps</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil-free diaphragm vacuum pumps market consists of different types of pumps that do not require the use of oil for operation. Claw vacuum pumps utilize two claw-shaped rotors that create a vacuum through the compression and expansion of gas. Screw vacuum pumps use two interlocking screws to create a vacuum by trapping and compressing gas. Scroll vacuum pumps use an orbiting scroll and a stationary scroll to generate a vacuum by compressing gas radially. Other types of pumps in this market include rotary vane and piston pumps, which operate on different principles to create a vacuum.</p></p>
