<p><strong>The Car Sealed Lead-acid Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AGM Battery</li><li>Gel Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Car Sealed Lead-acid Battery market consists of two major types: AGM Battery and Gel Battery. AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) batteries contain fiberglass mats that hold the electrolyte solution, providing excellent vibration resistance and high power output. These batteries are commonly used in vehicles with high electrical demand, such as luxury cars. On the other hand, Gel batteries feature silica-based electrolytes that turn into a gel-like substance, reducing the risk of acid leakage and providing enhanced cycle life. Gel batteries are often used in vehicles that require deep cycling, like golf carts and electric wheelchairs.</p></p>
