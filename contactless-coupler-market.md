There are two main types of contactless couplers: inductive couplers and capacitive couplers. Inductive couplers utilize magnetic fields to transfer energy or information, whereas capacitive couplers employ electrical fields for the same purpose. The choice between these two types depends on factors such as range, efficiency, and compatibility with specific applications. Both inductive and capacitive couplers offer the advantage of avoiding physical contact, enabling safer and more convenient operation in various industries.</p></p>
