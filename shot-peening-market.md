<p><p>Shot peening is a process used to enhance the fatigue life of metal components by bombarding them with small spherical particles. The shot peening market can be categorized into two types: automatic and semi-automatic. </p><p>Automatic shot peening involves the use of machines that perform the entire shot peening process automatically, from loading and unloading components to controlling the shot flow. This type of market is preferred for high-volume production and requires minimal manual intervention.</p><p>On the other hand, semi-automatic shot peening involves machines that require some level of operator intervention. The operator loads and unloads components, controls shot flow, and monitors the process. This market type is suitable for applications with lower production volumes or when process control flexibility is desired.</p></p>
