<p><p>Fresh beef packaging market types include Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP), Vacuum Skin Packaging (VSP), Vacuum Thermoformed Packaging (VTP), and Others. MAP involves replacing the natural atmospheric air with a mixture of gases to extend shelf life. VSP envelops the product using a tight film, offering an appealing display while increasing shelf life. VTP utilizes a vacuum to form a mold around the product, optimizing protection during transportation and storage. Other packaging types may include trays, bags, or pouches designed to maintain freshness and preserve the quality of fresh beef.</p></p>
