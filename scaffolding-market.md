<p><p>The scaffolding market can be categorized into different types based on their design and construction. Frame scaffolding is a commonly used type that consists of vertical frames connected by cross braces. Fastener scaffold employs bolts and clamps for secure attachment. Bowl-buckle scaffold uses bowl-shaped joints and pins to hold the structure together. Others in the market include systems like mobile scaffolds, bamboo scaffolds, and suspension scaffolds. Each type offers unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific requirements of a construction project.</p></p>
