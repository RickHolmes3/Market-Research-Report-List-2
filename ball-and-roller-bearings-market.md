<p><p>Ball bearings and roller bearings are types of bearings used in various industries. Ball bearings consist of balls placed between two rings, allowing for smooth rotation with a low friction coefficient. They are commonly used in machinery and automobiles. On the other hand, roller bearings use cylindrical rollers instead of balls, providing higher load-carrying capacity and tackling heavier loads. They are frequently utilized in heavy-duty applications such as construction equipment and large industrial machinery. Both types of bearings play a crucial role in enhancing efficiency and reducing friction in mechanical systems.</p></p>
