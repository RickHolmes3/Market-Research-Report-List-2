<p><p>The Bubble Level Indicator market consists of two types: Non-contact Type and Contact Type. The Non-contact Type refers to bubble level indicators that do not require physical contact with the object being measured. These types usually use electronic sensors or laser technology to assess the level. On the other hand, Contact Type bubble level indicators rely on physical contact with the object, typically using a liquid-filled tube with a bubble that accurately shows the level. Each type caters to various applications and user preferences in terms of accuracy, ease of use, and versatility.</p></p>
