<p><p>Bi-directional amplifiers (BDAs) are communication devices used to enhance signal strength in radio frequency (RF) systems. The market for BDAs can be categorized into two types: full-duplex and half-duplex. Full-duplex BDAs allow simultaneous transmission and reception of signals, enabling two-way communication at the same time. On the other hand, half-duplex BDAs only allow one-way communication at a time, with the ability to switch between transmitting and receiving signals. Both types of BDAs play crucial roles in improving signal quality and expanding the coverage area in RF systems.</p></p>
