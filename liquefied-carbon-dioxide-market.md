<p><p>Liquefied Carbon Dioxide (CO2) is a colorless, odorless, non-flammable gas that can be easily converted into a liquid state by increasing pressure or decreasing temperature. The increasing demand for CO2 in various industries such as food and beverage, healthcare, oil and gas, and agriculture is a key driver for market growth. The healthcare industry utilizes CO2 for laparoscopic surgeries, cryotherapy, and in the production of medical gases. Additionally, the oil and gas industry relies on CO2 for enhanced oil recovery (EOR) and as a propellant in fire extinguisher systems. The agriculture sector uses CO2 for greenhouse cultivation to enhance plant growth.</p><p>Another market trend in the Liquefied Carbon Dioxide Market is the increasing focus on sustainability and reducing greenhouse gas emissions.
