<p><strong>The Cutting Tool Blade Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The cutting tool blade market can be categorized into various types based on the materials used. Carbide blades are made from a mixture of tungsten carbide and cobalt and are known for their durability and resistance to heat. CBN (Cubic Boron Nitride) blades are made from a synthetic material that is extremely hard and suitable for cutting hard materials. Ceramic blades are made from a non-metallic material that is highly resistant to corrosion and wear. Other types of blades can include diamond blades, high-speed steel blades, and bi-metal blades, among others.</p></p>
