<p><p>The Industrial Machinery Turbocharger Market consists of two types: Mono Turbo and Twin Turbo. The Mono Turbo system involves a single turbocharger, which is most commonly used in smaller and medium-sized engines. It helps to increase the engine's power output by forcing more air into the combustion chamber. On the other hand, the Twin Turbo system utilizes two turbochargers to achieve optimal performance in larger engines. This setup allows for better control over the airflow, enhancing the engine's power and efficiency.</p></p>
