<p><p>A carbon powder forming machine is a mechanical device used to shape and mold carbon powder into desired forms such as briquettes, pellets, or blocks. The resulting products can then be used for various applications such as energy production, metallurgy, and chemistry.</p><p>The carbon powder forming machine market is witnessing significant growth and is expected to continue to expand at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 9.3% during the forecast period.
