Mid-Range speakers focus on producing mid-range frequencies, delivering clear vocals and instruments. Tweeters handle high-frequency sounds, improving audio clarity and detail. Woofer speakers produce low-frequency sounds and are essential for a rich bass response. These different types cater to diverse automotive audio needs, providing a well-rounded and immersive listening experience.</p></p>
