Disc harrow blades are used in farming equipment to break up and level soil, preparing it for planting. They have a flat, circular design with sharp edges for efficient cutting. On the other hand, disc plough blades are designed for more intensive tasks, such as deep ploughing and turning over soil. They typically have a more curved shape and are used to create furrows in the ground for planting crops.</p></p>
