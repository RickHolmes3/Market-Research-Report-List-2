<p><p>Ultrasonic heat meters are devices used to measure heat consumption in various applications. The market includes two main types: Integrated Heat Meters and Split Heat Meters. Integrated Heat Meters combine the flow sensor and calculator in one unit, making installation and operation easier. These meters are commonly used in residential and small commercial applications. Split Heat Meters, on the other hand, separate the flow sensor and calculator into two units, allowing for greater flexibility in installation and providing accurate measurements in large commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
