<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Sensor Heads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrinsic</li><li>Extrinsic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic sensor heads market is categorized into two types: intrinsic and extrinsic. Intrinsic fiber optic sensor heads are designed to measure physical quantities such as temperature, pressure, or strain directly along the optical fiber. They utilize the fiber itself as the sensing element, offering a compact and lightweight solution. Extrinsic fiber optic sensor heads, on the other hand, employ an external sensing element, such as a reflective surface, to detect and measure the desired physical parameters. This allows for more flexibility in sensor design and applications.</p></p>
