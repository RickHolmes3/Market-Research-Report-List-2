<p><p>The handheld laser cleaning machine market includes two main types of machines: Continuous Wave (CW) and Pulse handheld laser cleaning machines. CW handheld lasers emit a continuous beam of laser light, providing a steady and consistent cleaning power. On the other hand, Pulse handheld lasers emit short pulses of laser light, delivering high peak power for intense cleaning. Both types of machines are used for various cleaning applications, but CW lasers are preferred for delicate surfaces, while pulse lasers are ideal for removing tough contaminants or rust.</p></p>
