<p><p>Adhesives play a crucial role in optical path link-up applications, such as connecting optical fibers or other components in optical systems. There are two main types of adhesives used in this market: acrylate and epoxy. </p><p>Acrylate adhesives are known for their fast curing time and high optical transparency. They are commonly used for temporary or non-permanent link-ups as they provide easy reworkability. On the other hand, epoxy adhesives offer excellent bond strength and long-term durability. They are preferred for permanent link-ups where stability and reliability are essential. Both types meet the specific requirements of the optical path link-up market but cater to different use cases.</p></p>
