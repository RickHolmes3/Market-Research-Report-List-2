<p><p>Magnesium Oxide Wallboard is available in three market types based on thickness: thin, medium, and thick. Thin wallboards typically have a thickness of less than 6mm and are suitable for applications where weight and space are major concerns, such as wall partitions. Medium wallboards have a thickness range of 6-10mm and are commonly used for ceilings, fire barriers, and decorative purposes. Thick wallboards, with a thickness of more than 10mm, provide enhanced strength and impact resistance, making them suitable for applications where robustness is required, such as exterior walls and flooring.</p></p>
