<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>PVC foam for wind turbine market comprises two main types: PVC crosslinked foams and PVC non-crosslinked foams. PVC crosslinked foams are created through a process that chemically bonds the polymer chains, resulting in a more durable and rigid foam with excellent mechanical properties. They find applications in structural components of wind turbines due to their strength and ability to withstand harsh conditions. In contrast, PVC non-crosslinked foams are not chemically bonded, yielding a more flexible foam with good cushioning properties, making them suitable for insulation and soundproofing purposes in wind turbines.</p></p>
