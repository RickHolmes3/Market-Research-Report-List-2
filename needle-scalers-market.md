<p><p>Needle scalers are handheld tools used to remove rust, paint, and other surface coatings from metals. They come in two main types: straight and pistol grip. Straight needle scalers have a long, straight body that provides a better reach and balance, making them suitable for larger surfaces and overhead work. Pistol grip needle scalers have a shorter, curved body that offers better control and maneuverability, making them ideal for smaller or intricate areas. Both types are widely used in industries such as automotive, construction, and shipbuilding for surface preparation and cleaning tasks.</p></p>
