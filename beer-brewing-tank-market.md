<p><p>The beer brewing tank market consists of two main types: fermentation tanks and storage tanks. Fermentation tanks are used during the fermentation process, where yeast converts sugars into alcohol and carbon dioxide. They provide an ideal environment for yeast to thrive and produce the desired flavors and aromas in the beer. On the other hand, storage tanks are used to store the finished beer before packaging or distribution. These tanks ensure proper temperature and pressure control, maintaining the beer's quality and freshness until it reaches the consumer.</p></p>
