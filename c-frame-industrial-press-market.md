<p><p>C-frame industrial presses are available in three main market types: fully-automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. In the fully-automatic market, these presses are designed to operate without any human intervention, utilizing computerized systems to control the entire press cycle. In the semi-automatic market, operators are responsible for certain aspects of the press cycle, such as loading and unloading parts, while the rest of the process is automated. In the manual market, operators have complete control over the press cycle and perform all tasks manually without any automation.</p></p>
