<p><p>Papaya powder is available in two market types: organic and non-organic. Organic papaya powder is derived from papayas grown without the use of synthetic pesticides, fertilizers, or genetically modified organisms (GMOs). This type of powder is produced using natural farming methods, ensuring the absence of chemical residues and promoting environmental sustainability. On the other hand, non-organic papaya powder is made using conventionally grown papayas, which may involve synthetic chemicals during cultivation. Consumers who prioritize health, sustainability, and natural farming practices may prefer organic papaya powder, while others may opt for the more readily available non-organic option.</p></p>
