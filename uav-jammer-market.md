<p><p>The UAV jammer market comprises three main types: ground-based, hand-held, and UAV-based systems. Ground-based jammers are fixed solutions that are typically deployed in strategic locations to disrupt communication between the unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) and its controller. Hand-held jammers offer a portable and compact option for disrupting UAV signals, allowing individuals to counter UAV threats while on the move. UAV-based jammers are installed directly onto the UAV platform, enabling the unmanned aircraft itself to jam signals and neutralize potential threats during flight. Each type serves a unique purpose in countering malicious UAV activities.</p></p>
