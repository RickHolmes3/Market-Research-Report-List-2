<p><ul><li>Class A Transponder</li><li>Class B Transponder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Satellite-based Automatic Identification Systems (S-AIS) market consists of two main types of transponders: Class A and Class B. Class A transponders are designed for commercial vessels and provide comprehensive data such as identification, position, route, speed, and more. They are mandatory for larger ships and are known for their high accuracy and reliability. On the other hand, Class B transponders are smaller and usually used by leisure boats and smaller vessels. They offer basic identification and position information at a lower cost compared to Class A transponders.</p></p>
