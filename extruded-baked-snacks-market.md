<p><p>Extruded baked snacks come in two main market types: hot extrusion and cold extrusion. In hot extrusion, the snack dough is cooked at a high temperature during the extrusion process, resulting in a crunchy and crispy texture. On the other hand, in cold extrusion, the dough is processed without any heat, leading to a softer and chewier texture. Both types offer unique characteristics that cater to different consumer preferences and provide a diverse range of snack options in the market.</p></p>
