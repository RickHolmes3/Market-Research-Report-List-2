<p><p>The refractory alumina raw materials market consists of various types, including calcined alumina, fused alumina, tabular alumina, and others. Calcined alumina is produced by heating alumina hydrate to high temperatures, resulting in a fine powder used for refractory applications. Fused alumina is made by melting alumina in an electric arc furnace and used for abrasive and refractory purposes. Tabular alumina is produced by sintering alumina aggregates and finds application in refractories. The "others" category includes specialty alumina raw materials like white fused alumina and brown fused alumina, which serve specific industry needs.</p></p>
