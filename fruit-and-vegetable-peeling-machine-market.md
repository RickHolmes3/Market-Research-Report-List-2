<p><p>The fruit and vegetable peeling machine market consists of two main types: fully automatic peeling machines and semi-automatic peeling machines. Fully automatic peeling machines are designed to handle the entire peeling process without any human intervention. They are equipped with advanced technology and can process large quantities of fruits and vegetables efficiently. On the other hand, semi-automatic peeling machines require some level of human involvement during the peeling process. While they offer automation for certain tasks, such as cutting or slicing, manual intervention is needed for loading and unloading the produce.</p></p>
