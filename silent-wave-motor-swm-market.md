<p><p>The Silent Wave Motor (SWM) is a type of motor used in camera lenses to achieve fast and quiet autofocus. There are three market types of SWM: the Travelling Wave Type, Standing Wave Type, and Vibrating Reed Type. In the Travelling Wave Type, the motor generates a wave that travels along a coil to drive the focusing element. The Standing Wave Type creates a standing wave pattern in the coil, while the Vibrating Reed Type uses a vibrating reed to drive the focusing element. All three types aim to provide efficient and silent autofocus functionality.</p></p>
