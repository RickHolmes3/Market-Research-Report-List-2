<p><p>Capsule fillers are devices used for filling empty capsules with medication or supplements. Manual (hand-held) capsule fillers are operated by hand and are suitable for small-scale production. Semi-automatic capsule fillers have semi-automated processes like capsule separation and dosage adjustment. They are suitable for medium-scale production. Automatic capsule fillers are fully automated machines capable of high-speed production with advanced features like capsule polishing and sorting. They are ideal for large-scale production. Each type of capsule filler offers varying levels of efficiency, capacity, and automation to meet different production needs.</p></p>
