<p><p>Bismaleimide (BMI) Prepreg is a type of composite material that consists of a bismaleimide resin matrix reinforced with fibers. The market for BMI Prepreg can be divided into two types: Unidirectional BMI Prepreg and Fabric BMI Prepreg. </p><p>Unidirectional BMI Prepreg is composed of parallel fibers embedded in a BMI resin matrix, providing high strength and stiffness in one direction. It is commonly used in applications where load-bearing capability is crucial, such as aerospace and automotive industries.</p><p>Fabric BMI Prepreg, on the other hand, is made up of woven or stitched fibers infused with a BMI resin matrix. This type offers improved impact resistance and durability compared to the unidirectional type, making it suitable for diverse applications including sporting goods and marine industries.</p></p>
