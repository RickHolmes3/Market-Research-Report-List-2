<p><p>Servo Hydraulic Actuator market is divided into two main types: Servo Hydraulic Dynamic Actuator and Servo Hydraulic Static Actuator. A Servo Hydraulic Dynamic Actuator is designed for applications that require fast and precise movements. It is commonly used in robotics, flight simulators, and motion control systems. On the other hand, a Servo Hydraulic Static Actuator is used for applications that require holding force or constant position control. It is typically employed in industrial machinery, material handling equipment, and construction machinery. Both types of actuators play essential roles in various industries, providing efficient and reliable control.</p></p>
