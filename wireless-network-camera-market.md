<p><ul><li>Cellular Network</li><li>Wifi Network</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless network camera market consists of two main types: cellular network and WiFi network cameras. Cellular network cameras use cellular connections, such as 3G or 4G/LTE, to transmit video signals, allowing for remote monitoring even in areas without WiFi coverage. On the other hand, WiFi network cameras operate through WiFi connections, enabling users to access live video feeds and recordings through local networks or the internet. Both types offer convenient surveillance solutions, with cellular cameras suitable for outdoor or remote locations, while WiFi cameras are ideal for indoor use and local network access.</p></p>
