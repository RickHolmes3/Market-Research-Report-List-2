<p><ul><li>Square Port</li><li>Circular Port</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Orthomode Transducer (OMT) is a device used in communication systems for separating or combining two different frequency polarizations. Two common market types of OMTs are Square Port and Circular Port. In Square Port OMT, the input and output ports are square-shaped, allowing for easy integration into rectangular waveguides. Circular Port OMT, on the other hand, has circular input and output ports, making it suitable for circular waveguides or antenna systems. Each market type serves specific applications, providing flexibility and compatibility in different communication setups.</p></p>
