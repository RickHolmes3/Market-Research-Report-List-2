<p><p>The non-woven cloth market for medical masks consists of two types: single layer melt blown fabric and double layers melt blown fabric. Single layer melt blown fabric is made by extruding melted polymer through small nozzles to form thin fibers. These fibers are then randomly arranged and bonded together to create a fabric that provides filtration and breathability in medical masks. Double layer melt blown fabric is created using two layers of single layer fabric, providing enhanced filtration efficiency and protection against harmful particles.</p></p>
