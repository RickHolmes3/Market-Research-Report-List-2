<p><strong>The Aviation Snip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight-cut Snip</li><li>Offset Snip</li><li>Vertical Snip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aviation snip market consists of three main types: straight-cut snips, offset snips, and vertical snips. Straight-cut snips are the most common type and are used for straight cuts on sheet metal. Offset snips have a handle that is slightly angled, allowing for easier cutting in tight spaces or overhead. Vertical snips have blades that are oriented vertically, perfect for making cuts parallel to the edge of the sheet metal. Each type of snip serves a different purpose, providing options for various cutting needs in the aviation industry.</p></p>
