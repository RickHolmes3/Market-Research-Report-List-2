<p><p>The avalanche beacon market consists of two main types: digital beacons and analog beacons. Digital beacons utilize advanced technology to provide more accurate and faster search capabilities in locating buried avalanche victims. They often incorporate multiple antennas and offer features such as multiple burial indication and marking. On the other hand, analog beacons rely on basic radio frequency technology and manual search techniques. They require more skill and experience to use effectively but are generally considered more affordable. Both types are crucial safety devices for backcountry enthusiasts and professionals venturing into avalanche-prone areas.</p></p>
