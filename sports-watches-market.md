<p><p>Sports watches come in two main market types: mechanical watches and electronic watches. On the other hand, electronic watches use quartz technology and batteries to accurately track time, offering a more affordable and convenient choice. Both types cater to the needs of athletes and sports enthusiasts, but differ in their mechanisms and cost. Customers can choose between the traditional and elegant appeal of mechanical watches or the modern and efficient features of electronic watches.</p></p>
