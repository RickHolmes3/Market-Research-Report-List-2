<p><p>The Automatic Hardness Testers market consists of different types of hardness testers, including the Rockwell Hardness Tester and Brinell Hardness Tester. Rockwell Hardness Tester is a commonly used method to determine the hardness of materials by measuring the depth of indentation caused by a load. On the other hand, the Brinell Hardness Tester determines hardness through measuring the diameter of an impression left by a spherical indenter. Both testers provide accurate and precise hardness measurements, catering to various industries' needs.</p></p>
