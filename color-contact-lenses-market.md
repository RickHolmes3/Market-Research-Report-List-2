<p><p>Color contact lenses come in different types to suit various needs. Visibility tint lenses have a light color tint added to them, allowing wearers to easily locate and handle them. Enhancement tint lenses are designed to enhance the wearer's natural eye color, giving it a subtle boost. These lenses are translucent and provide a more natural look. Opaque tint lenses, on the other hand, completely change the color of the wearer's eyes. These lenses are suitable for those who want a dramatic change in eye color.</p></p>
