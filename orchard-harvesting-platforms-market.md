<p><p>Orchard harvesting platforms are specialized machines used for efficiently harvesting fruit in orchards. There are two main types: self-propelled platforms and towed platforms. Self-propelled platforms are standalone machines equipped with engines to move themselves around the orchard independently. Towed platforms, on the other hand, are pulled by a tractor or other vehicle to move within the orchard. Both types are designed to enable farmers to harvest fruit more efficiently by providing a stable working platform at varying heights.</p></p>
