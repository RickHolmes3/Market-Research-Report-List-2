<p><p>Garbage collection vehicles can be classified into two types: manual and semi-automatic. Manual garbage collection vehicles require workers to physically lift and place trash into the truck, making it a labor-intensive process. On the other hand, semi-automatic garbage collection vehicles are equipped with hydraulic systems that lift and empty waste containers into the truck automatically. This technology reduces the physical strain on workers and increases efficiency. Both types of vehicles play a crucial role in waste management, ensuring the cleanliness and hygiene of our communities.</p></p>
