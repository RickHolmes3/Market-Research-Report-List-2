Vertical basket centrifuges have a vertical axis of rotation and are suitable for processes requiring high rotational speeds and large volumes. They are often used in mining, chemical, and pharmaceutical industries. On the other hand, horizontal basket centrifuges have a horizontal axis of rotation and are ideal for processes requiring continuous operation and gentle product handling. They find applications in food processing, wastewater treatment, and oil industries.
