<p><strong>The Infrared Photoelectric Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Infrared Photoelectric Sensor market can be broadly categorized into two types: active and passive sensors. Active infrared sensors emit their own infrared light and measure the reflection or interruption of this light to detect an object or presence. They are commonly used in motion detection systems. On the other hand, passive infrared sensors detect the natural infrared emissions from objects and use changes in these emissions to identify movement or presence. They are commonly found in burglar alarms and automatic lighting systems.</p></p>
