<p><p>Micro electric vehicles refer to small-sized electric vehicles that are designed for short-distance urban commuting. There are two main market types in this segment: pure electric and hybrid electric. Pure electric vehicles are solely powered by electricity and do not have an internal combustion engine. They rely on rechargeable batteries to operate. On the other hand, hybrid electric vehicles combine an electric motor with an internal combustion engine. They can run on both electricity and gasoline, offering greater range and flexibility compared to pure electric vehicles.</p></p>
