<p><p>High-precision gear reducers refer to mechanical devices used to control the speed and torque of motors. RV precision reduction gears and harmonic precision reduction gears are two types found in the market. RV precision reduction gears utilize roller gears and provide high torque, smooth motion, and compact design suitable for robotic applications. On the other hand, harmonic precision reduction gears leverage flexible metal bands to generate smooth movement, making them ideal for applications that require high precision and accuracy, such as robotics, aerospace, and medical equipment.</p></p>
