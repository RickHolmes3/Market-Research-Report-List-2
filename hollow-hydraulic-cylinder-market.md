<p><p>A single acting hollow hydraulic cylinder is a type of hydraulic cylinder that only applies force in one direction, usually through the use of hydraulic fluid being pumped into the cylinder to extend the piston. This type of cylinder is commonly used in applications where there is a need for a pushing or lifting force. On the other hand, a double acting hollow hydraulic cylinder can apply force in both directions, as hydraulic fluid is pumped into one end to extend the piston and then into the other end to retract it. This type of cylinder is often used in applications that require both pushing and pulling forces.</p></p>
