<p><p>Dengue Testing Market comprises various types of tests to diagnose dengue fever. ELISA-Based Tests use Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay to detect dengue virus antigens or antibodies. RT-PCR Based Tests use Reverse Transcription Polymerase Chain Reaction to identify viral RNA in blood samples. Dengue IgG/IgM Rapid Test detects IgG and IgM antibodies in the blood, indicating the presence of dengue infection. These tests play a crucial role in identifying dengue cases promptly, enabling timely treatment and prevention measures.</p></p>
