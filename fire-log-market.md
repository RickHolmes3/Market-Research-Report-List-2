<p><p>Fire log market consists of three main types: compressed sawdust made logs, sawdust and paraffin made logs, and renewable biomass fibers made logs. Compressed sawdust logs are produced by compressing sawdust, resulting in dense and efficient logs that burn slowly and produce little smoke. Sawdust and paraffin logs are created by blending sawdust with paraffin wax, offering clean and consistent combustion. Renewable biomass fibers logs, on the other hand, are made from a combination of organic materials like agricultural residues, providing an eco-friendly alternative while still offering efficient heat and low emission levels.</p></p>
