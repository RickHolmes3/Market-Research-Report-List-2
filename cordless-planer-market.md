<p><p>A cordless planer is a portable power tool used for smoothing and shaping wooden surfaces. Unlike traditional planers that require a power cord or a direct power source, cordless planers are battery-operated, offering greater mobility and convenience.</p><p>The cordless planer market has witnessed significant growth in recent years. The rise in construction activities and the booming real estate industry have also contributed to the market growth.</p><p>Additionally, technological advancements have led to the introduction of cordless planers with improved features and performance.
