<p><p>The metallic craft paint market can be categorized into two types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based metallic craft paint is made with solvents as a base, which helps in dissolving the pigments and binders. It provides a glossy and durable finish on various surfaces and is often used for outdoor applications. On the other hand, water-based metallic craft paint is formulated with water as a base, making it easy to clean up and less harmful to the environment. It dries quickly and is commonly used for indoor crafts and artworks.</p></p>
