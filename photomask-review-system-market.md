<p><strong>The Photomask Review System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photomask Review System market comprises Die to Die (DD) Method and Die to Database (DB) Method. In the DD Method, the system compares photomask images with each other to ensure accurate reproduction of complex patterns on the chip. It helps identify errors or discrepancies between masks. On the other hand, the DB Method compares the photomask images with an established reference database. It ensures the photomasks are faithfully reproduced as intended. Both methods play crucial roles in the semiconductor industry by improving quality control and minimizing errors in chip manufacturing.</p></p>
