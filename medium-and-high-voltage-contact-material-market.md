<p><p>Medium and high voltage contact materials are categorized into three types: CuCr contact material, CuW contact material, and Others. CuCr contact material, made of copper and chromium, offers excellent electrical conductivity and high thermal and mechanical stability. CuW contact material, composed of copper and tungsten, provides high arc resistance and strong resistance to welding and erosion. The Others category includes contact materials made of different compositions, such as tungsten-copper or silver-based alloys, which possess unique properties suited for specific applications. These materials are widely used in electrical power transmission and distribution systems.</p></p>
