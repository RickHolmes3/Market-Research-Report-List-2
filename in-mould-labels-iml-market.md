<p><p>In-Mould Labels (IML) are a type of labeling technique used in various markets including Injection Molding, Extrusion- Blow Molding, and Thermoforming. In the Injection Molding market, IML involves placing labels in the mold cavity before injecting the material, resulting in a label that is seamlessly fused with the product. In Extrusion- Blow Molding, labels are inserted into the mold and blown with plastic to create hollow products with integrated labels. Thermoforming involves placing labels on plastic sheets, which are then molded and cut into desired shapes.</p></p>
