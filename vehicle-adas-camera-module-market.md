<p><p>The Vehicle ADAS Camera Module market consists of CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) Cameras and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) Cameras. CCD cameras use a sensor technology that converts light into electrical charge, providing high-quality images with low noise levels. On the other hand, CMOS cameras use a different sensor technology that converts light into electrical current, leading to lower power consumption and faster processing speed. Both camera types play a crucial role in the ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems) market, providing necessary visual information for features like lane departure warning, collision avoidance, and autonomous driving capabilities.</p></p>
