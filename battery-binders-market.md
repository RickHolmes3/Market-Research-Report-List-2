<p><p>Battery binders are essential components used in the manufacturing of batteries. Anode binders are used in lithium-ion batteries to connect the anode electrode materials, providing stability and preventing material detachment during charging and discharging processes. Cathode binders, on the other hand, are used to connect cathode electrode materials, ensuring their adhesion to the current collector and improving the overall performance and efficiency of the battery. Both binders play crucial roles in battery production by enhancing electrode performance and longevity.</p></p>
