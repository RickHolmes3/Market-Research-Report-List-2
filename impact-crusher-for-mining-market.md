<p><p>The Impact Crusher for Mining Market comprises two main types: Horizontal Impact Crusher and Vertical Impact Crusher. Horizontal Impact Crushers have a horizontal shaft and use impact energy to break the material. They provide a consistent reduction in size and are commonly used for soft materials like limestone. On the other hand, Vertical Impact Crushers have a vertical shaft and use a different rock-on-rock crushing method. They are more suitable for harder materials like granite or ores. Both types play a crucial role in the mining industry by efficiently breaking down large rocks into smaller sizes for further processing.</p></p>
