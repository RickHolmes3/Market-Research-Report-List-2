<p><p>The seaweed fertilizer market is classified based on four types of production processes. Chemical hydrolysis involves the use of chemicals to break down seaweed into a fertilizer. Physics extraction method refers to the process of physically extracting nutrients from seaweed through processes like pressure, heat, and solvents. Enzymolysis process utilizes enzymes to extract nutrients from seaweed. Lastly, the biological fermentation process involves the fermentation of seaweed by beneficial microorganisms to convert it into a nutrient-rich fertilizer. These different methods offer varying benefits and options for producing seaweed fertilizers.</p></p>
