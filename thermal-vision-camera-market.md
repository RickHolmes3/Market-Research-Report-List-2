<p><strong>The Thermal Vision Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-wave Length Camera</li><li>Mid-wave Length Camera</li><li>Long-wave Length Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thermal Vision Camera Market is categorized into three types based on the length of the thermal waves detected by the camera. Short-wave length cameras detect the shortest thermal waves, typically used in military and security applications. Mid-wave length cameras can capture medium-length thermal waves and find use in industrial inspections, firefighting, and surveillance. Long-wave length cameras are sensitive to longer thermal waves and are commonly employed in medical imaging, research, and energy auditing. Each type serves specific industries and applications, offering different levels of accuracy, resolution, and temperature range.</p></p>
