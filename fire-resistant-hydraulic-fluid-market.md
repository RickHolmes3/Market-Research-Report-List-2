<p><p>Fire-resistant hydraulic fluids (FRHF) are specifically developed for applications where there is a high risk of fire. FRHF are categorized into four types: HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD, each offering different properties and applications. HFA fluids are water-based with additional additives for fire resistance. HFB fluids are water/glycol-based, providing higher temperature stability. HFC fluids are synthetic ester-based and exhibit excellent fire resistance and temperature stability. HFD fluids are the most advanced, combining synthetic esters and water to offer superior lubrication, high fire resistance, and optimal environmental friendliness.</p></p>
