<p><p>A Zero Liquid Discharge System (ZLD) is a water treatment process that aims to recover and reuse all water from industrial processes, leaving no liquid waste. There are two types of ZLD systems: Conventional ZLD System and Hybrid ZLD System. Conventional ZLD systems utilize physical and chemical processes, such as evaporation and crystallization, to eliminate liquid waste. Hybrid ZLD systems combine conventional methods with membrane-based technologies, like reverse osmosis, to enhance water recovery. Both systems help minimize environmental impact and conserve water resources in industrial sectors.</p></p>
