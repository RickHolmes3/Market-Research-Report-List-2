<p><p>The trailer refrigeration system market consists of two main types: single temperature and multi-temperature systems. Single temperature systems are designed to maintain a consistent temperature throughout the trailer, usually for transporting a single type of product. On the other hand, multi-temperature systems allow for the transportation of different products at varying temperatures within the same trailer. These systems are important for industries like food and pharmaceuticals where different goods require different temperature conditions during transportation.</p></p>
