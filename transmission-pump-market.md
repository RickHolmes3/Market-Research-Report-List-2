<p><p>The transmission pump market can be classified into two types: fixed displacement pump and variable displacement pump. Fixed displacement pumps maintain a constant flow rate, providing a consistent supply of fluid for transmission systems. On the other hand, variable displacement pumps allow the operator to adjust the flow rate according to the system's requirements, making them more efficient and versatile. Both types are essential components in transmission systems and are used in various industries such as automotive, construction, and agriculture.</p></p>
