<p><p>The infrared laser diodes market can be classified into different types based on spatial mode and package type. Spatial mode refers to the laser beam's distribution of power across its cross-sectional area. Single mode diodes emit a narrow, focused beam, while multimode diodes produce a broader beam. Package type categorizes diodes based on their structure and form factor. N type diodes are negatively charged, while P type diodes are positively charged. Understanding these classifications helps stakeholders in selecting the appropriate infrared laser diodes for their specific applications.</p></p>
