<p><p>Enamelled glass market types can be categorized as transparent, translucent, and opaque. Transparent enamelled glass allows light to pass through without significant distortion, providing clear visibility. Translucent enamelled glass allows diffused light to pass through but limits visibility, offering privacy while still allowing some light transmission. Opaque enamelled glass does not allow light to pass through, providing complete privacy. These different types cater to various applications, such as windows, doors, partitions, and decorative glass, where different levels of visibility and privacy are desired.</p></p>
