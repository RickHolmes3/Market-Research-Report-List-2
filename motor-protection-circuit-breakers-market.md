<p><p>Motor Protection Circuit Breakers (MPCBs) are electrical devices used to protect electric motors from short circuits, overloads, and other potential electrical faults. MPCBs are classified into two types based on the voltage level they are designed for: medium voltage and low voltage. </p><p>Medium voltage MPCBs are specifically used for motors operating at higher voltage levels, typically ranging from hundreds to thousands of volts. They provide enhanced protection for medium voltage motors against electrical faults, ensuring safe and reliable motor operations in industrial applications.</p><p>On the other hand, low voltage MPCBs are designed for motors operating at lower voltage levels, typically up to 1,000 volts. They offer similar protection capabilities as medium voltage MPCBs but are tailored for low voltage motors commonly found in residential, commercial, and small-scale industrial applications. These MPCBs safeguard motors from potential electrical faults, ensuring their longevity and preventing damage or downtime.</p></p>
