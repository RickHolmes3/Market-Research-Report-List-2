<p><p>The swimming pool cleaning machines market can be categorized into three types: robotic pool cleaners, suction pool cleaners, and pressure pool cleaners. Robotic pool cleaners are automated devices that navigate the pool surfaces, removing dirt and debris using their built-in brushes and filters. Suction pool cleaners operate by attaching to the pool's filtration system and moving around the pool, sucking up dirt and debris. Pressure pool cleaners use water pressure to drive the unit around the pool, effectively cleaning the surfaces by capturing dirt and debris.</p></p>
