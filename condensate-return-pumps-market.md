<p><p>Condensate return pumps are used in industrial settings to transfer condensed water (condensate) back to the boiler for reuse. There are two main types of condensate return pumps: electric pumps and steam pumps. Electric pumps use an electric motor to drive the pump, providing a reliable and efficient solution. Steam pumps, on the other hand, utilize the pressure of steam to create a vacuum that pulls the condensate back to the boiler. Both types of pumps serve the same purpose of returning condensate, but differ in their mechanisms for achieving this.</p></p>
