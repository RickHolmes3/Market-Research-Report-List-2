<p><p>The hybrid solar inverter market consists of two main types: single-phase and three-phase. Single-phase inverters are designed for residential or small commercial applications with single-phase power supply systems. They convert DC power generated by solar panels into the AC power used in households or businesses. On the other hand, three-phase inverters are suitable for larger commercial or industrial installations. They are designed to convert and manage the power generated by solar panels in three-phase power supply systems, which are commonly found in large-scale electricity distribution networks.</p></p>
