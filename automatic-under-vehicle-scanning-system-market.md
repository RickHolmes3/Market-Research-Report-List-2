<p><p>The Automatic Under Vehicle Scanning System (UVSS) market can be categorized into two main types: Fixed and Mobile. Fixed UVSS refers to the installation of the scanning system at a fixed location, typically at the entrance or exit of a facility such as airports, government buildings, or military bases. It allows for a constant, automated detection of any suspicious objects or activities underneath vehicles. On the other hand, Mobile UVSS involves a portable scanning system that can be deployed in various locations as needed. This flexibility enables security agencies or event organizers to quickly set up scanning capabilities wherever required.</p></p>
