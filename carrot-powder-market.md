<p><p>The Carrot Powder market encompasses two main types: Spray Drying Carrot Powder and Freeze-dried Carrot Powder. Spray drying involves the process of converting liquid carrot extract into powder form using hot air, resulting in a fine-textured powder that is easily soluble. Freeze-dried carrot powder, on the other hand, is obtained by freezing the carrots and then drying them using low temperature and reduced pressure. This process helps retain the original color, flavor, and nutritional properties of the carrots. Both types find applications in various industries, including food, beverages, and nutraceuticals.</p></p>
