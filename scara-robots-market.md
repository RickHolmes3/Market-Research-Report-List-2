<p><p>SCARA robots can be classified into two market types: Single Arm SCARA and Dual Arm SCARA. Single Arm SCARA robots consist of a single arm with rotational joints and a vertical axis, enabling them to perform operations in a two-dimensional workspace. They are typically used for tasks like assembly, pick-and-place, and inspection. Dual Arm SCARA robots, on the other hand, have two independent arms that allow them to work in a three-dimensional workspace. These robots find applications in more complex tasks such as intricate assembly, material handling, and collaborative manufacturing.</p></p>
