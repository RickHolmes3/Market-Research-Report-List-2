<p><p>Flanged resistance thermometers are types of temperature sensors that are used in various industries to measure temperature accurately. They are available in different configurations such as 2 wire, 3 wire, and 4 wire. </p><p>In a 2 wire configuration, the resistance thermometer uses two wires for both power supply and signal output. This configuration is simple and cost-effective but can be prone to measurement errors.</p><p>In a 3 wire configuration, the resistance thermometer uses three wires, with two wires for power supply and signal output, and the third wire for compensation. This compensates for the resistance of the wires, reducing measurement errors.</p><p>In a 4 wire configuration, the resistance thermometer uses four wires, with two wires for power supply and two wires for signal output. This configuration eliminates any errors due to the resistance of the wires and provides highly accurate temperature measurements.</p></p>
