It works by passing an electric arc through a gas such as air or nitrogen, which ionizes the gas and creates plasma. The plasma is then directed towards the material to be cut, melting or vaporizing it while a high-velocity jet of gas blows away the molten material.</p><p>The current outlook of the air plasma cutter market is positive, with a steady growth expected in the coming years.
