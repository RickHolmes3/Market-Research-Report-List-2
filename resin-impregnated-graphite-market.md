Plates are flat sheets of resin-impregnated graphite that find application in electrical and thermal insulation, as well as in various industries requiring high temperature resistance. Blocks are solid structures of resin-impregnated graphite, often used as electrodes for electrical discharge machining and as components in the glass and semiconductor industries.</p></p>
