<p><p>UV germicidal bulbs, also known as UV-C bulbs, are specialized light bulbs that emit ultraviolet (UV) radiation in the germicidal range of 253.7 nanometers (nm). These bulbs are designed to kill or inactivate microorganisms such as bacteria, viruses, and mold spores by damaging their DNA or RNA, rendering them unable to replicate. UV germicidal bulbs are commonly used in various applications including water purification, air sterilization, surface disinfection, and food and beverage processing.</p><p>The UV germicidal bulbs market is expected to experience steady growth during the forecast period.
