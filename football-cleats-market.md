<p><p>Football cleats, or boots, are designed for different playing surfaces to enhance performance and prevent injuries. There are three main market types: SG (Soft Ground), AG (Artificial Ground), and FG (Firm Ground). SG cleats feature longer, detachable studs for wet and muddy conditions to provide better traction. AG cleats have shorter, molded studs specifically designed for artificial turf, providing optimal grip while reducing strain on joints. FG cleats, with shorter, more numerous, molded studs, are suitable for firm, natural grass surfaces to enable quick movements and stability. Each type corresponds to specific field conditions to optimize players' performance.</p></p>
