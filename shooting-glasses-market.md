<p><p>The shooting glasses market consists of two main types: transparent lenses and colored lenses. Transparent lenses provide clear vision and are suitable for shooting in low-light conditions or indoor ranges. They offer protection against debris, UV rays, and glare. On the other hand, colored lenses come in various shades like yellow, orange, and brown. These lenses enhance contrast, improve depth perception, and block blue light. They are commonly used for shooting in bright daylight or in outdoor environments with different lighting conditions. Both types of lenses aim to ensure safety and optimize visibility for shooters.</p></p>
