<p><p>Commercial central air conditioning systems come in various types to cater to different needs. Water-cooled air conditioning is a system that uses water to remove heat from the building and requires a separate cooling tower. Multi-split packaged air conditioning consists of multiple indoor units connected to a single outdoor unit, offering flexibility and improved cooling efficiency. In comparison, single-split packaged air conditioning has one indoor unit connected to an outdoor unit. Other types of commercial central air conditioning systems include variable refrigerant flow (VRF) systems, rooftop units, and ductless mini-split systems.</p></p>
