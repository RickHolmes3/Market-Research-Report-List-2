<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Lug butterfly valves are available in various types based on their end connections. </p><p>1. Flanged Ends: These valves have flanges on their ends, allowing them to be bolted directly to the pipeline. Flanged ends provide a strong connection and are commonly used in industrial applications.</p><p>2. Threaded Ends: Valves with threaded ends can be screwed directly onto the pipeline. They are commonly used in smaller pipes or where frequent maintenance is required.</p><p>3. Socket Ends: These valves have sockets on their ends, allowing them to be joined to the pipe by solvent welding. Socket ends provide a leak-proof connection and are commonly used in PVC or plastic pipes.</p><p>4. Butt Welded Ends: These valves are welded directly to the pipeline using butt welding technique. Butt welded ends provide a strong and permanent connection and are commonly used in high-pressure or critical applications.</p></p>
