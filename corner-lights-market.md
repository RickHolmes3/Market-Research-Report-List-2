<p><p>Corner lights are an essential component of a vehicle's lighting system. The market for corner lights can be divided into three types: Halogen/Incandescent, Xenon/HID, and LED. Halogen/Incandescent corner lights use a tungsten filament enclosed in a glass envelope and emit a warm white light. Xenon/HID corner lights produce a bright blue-white light by generating an electric arc between two electrodes encased in a xenon-filled bulb. LED corner lights use light-emitting diodes that emit bright, energy-efficient light. Each type of corner light offers different features and benefits for vehicle lighting.</p></p>
