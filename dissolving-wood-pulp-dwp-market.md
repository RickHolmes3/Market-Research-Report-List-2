Alkali Wood Pulp is produced using a chemical process involving wood chips and an alkali solution, resulting in a high-quality pulp used in the production of rayon and other cellulose derivatives. Sulfate Wood Pulp, on the other hand, is manufactured through a different chemical process using sulfate, and it is commonly used in paper production.
