<p><p>The circulating water coolers market consists of various types of chillers. Reciprocating chillers use a piston-driven compressor to cool the water. Centrifugal chillers utilize a rotating impeller to cool the water using a refrigerant. Screw chillers use a rotating screw compressor to cool the water. Apart from these three main types, there are also other chillers available in the market, such as absorption chillers, rotary chillers, and magnetic bearing chillers. Each type of chiller has its unique features and advantages, catering to different cooling requirements in various industries.</p></p>
