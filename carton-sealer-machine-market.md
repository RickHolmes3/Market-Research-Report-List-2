<p><p>The carton sealer machine market is classified into three types based on their functionality: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Automatic carton sealers are equipped with advanced technology and require minimal human intervention. They can seal a large number of cartons at a high speed, making them suitable for high-volume production. Semi-automatic carton sealers need human assistance to load the carton into the machine, but the sealing process is automated. Manual carton sealers require complete human involvement for each step of the sealing process. These machines are ideal for low-volume production or small operations.</p></p>
