<p><ul><li>Contactless Sensor</li><li>Piercing Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The needle sensor market consists of two main types: contactless sensor and piercing sensor. Contactless sensors are designed to measure various parameters without piercing the skin. They use technologies like infrared, magnetic, or optical sensors to measure vital signs or other health indicators. On the other hand, piercing sensors are used during medical procedures like blood collection or drug administration. These sensors have a needle-like structure that penetrates the skin to collect samples or deliver medication. Both types of sensors play crucial roles in healthcare, offering different functionalities depending on the application.</p></p>
