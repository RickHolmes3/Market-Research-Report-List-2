Negative Index Metamaterials (NIM) possess a negative refractive index, allowing for improved antenna performance. Single Negative (SNG) Metamaterials exhibit only negative values for certain material properties, offering enhanced antenna capabilities. Hyperbolic Metamaterials (HMM) have an indefinite permittivity or permeability, enabling improved control over electromagnetic wave propagation. These metamaterial markets aim to enhance communication antenna performance and functionality through advanced material properties.</p></p>
