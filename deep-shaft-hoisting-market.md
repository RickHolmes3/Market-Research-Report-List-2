<p><strong>The Deep Shaft Hoisting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Deep shaft hoisting is a crucial process in mining and industrial operations. There are different types of hoists used for this purpose. Friction hoists use friction between the hoist rope and the sheave to lift the load. Drum hoists wrap the hoist rope around a drum to lift the load. Blair Multi-rope hoists use several ropes connected to the load to distribute the weight evenly. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, and the choice depends on factors such as load capacity, speed, and safety requirements.</p></p>
