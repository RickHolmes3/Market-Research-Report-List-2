<p><p>Quartz Crystal Oscillators (QXOs) are devices used to generate precise oscillations in electronic circuits. They are classified into different types based on their capabilities. SPXOs (Standard Precision Crystal Oscillators) offer basic frequency stability. TCXOs (Temperature Compensated Crystal Oscillators) maintain stability in temperature-varying conditions. VCXOs (Voltage Controlled Crystal Oscillators) allow frequency adjustments through voltage control. OCXOs (Oven Controlled Crystal Oscillators) provide extreme stability by maintaining a constant temperature. The Quartz Crystal market encompasses the manufacturing and sales of these oscillators, meeting various electronic needs with reliable and accurate frequency generation.</p></p>
