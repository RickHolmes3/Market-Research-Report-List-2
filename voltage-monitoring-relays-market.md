<p><strong>The Voltage Monitoring Relays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Phase</li><li>Three-Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Voltage monitoring relays are designed to monitor and protect electrical systems from voltage fluctuations and faults. There are two main types of voltage monitoring relays based on the phase configuration: single-phase and three-phase. </p><p>Single-phase voltage monitoring relays are used in electrical systems where power is supplied through a single phase. They monitor the voltage level and can detect overvoltage or undervoltage conditions, triggering protective actions to prevent damage to equipment.</p><p>Three-phase voltage monitoring relays are employed in electrical systems where power is supplied through three phases. They monitor the voltage levels of each phase individually as well as the overall balance between phases. They can also detect phase sequence faults and provide protection against phase loss, ensuring the stability and safety of the electrical system.</p></p>
