<p><p>Laser Acousto-Optical Frequency Shifters (AOFS) are devices used in laser systems to shift the frequency of light. There are two types of AOFS markets: Free-Space AOFS and Fiber-Coupled AOFS. Free-Space AOFS uses open space to transmit and manipulate the light, making it suitable for applications that require high power and fast modulation. On the other hand, Fiber-Coupled AOFS utilizes optical fibers to transmit the light, allowing for compact and flexible integration into existing fiber optic systems. Both markets offer different advantages depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
