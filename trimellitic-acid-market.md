<p><p>Trimellitic acid is an organic compound with the molecular formula C9H6O6. It is a white crystalline solid that is insoluble in water but soluble in organic solvents. Trimellitic acid is primarily used as a raw material for the production of plasticizers, such as trimellitates, which are widely used in the manufacturing of PVC products, including wires, cables, and films. It is also used as a crosslinking agent in the production of epoxy resins and polyurethane coatings.</p><p>The current outlook of the trimellitic acid market is positive and is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
