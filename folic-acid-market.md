<p><p>Folic acid, also known as vitamin B9, is a synthetic form of folate, a water-soluble vitamin essential for various bodily functions. The growing prevalence of neural tube defects and other related disorders in newborns has propelled the demand for folic acid supplements among pregnant women. Additionally, folic acid is widely used in the food and beverage industry to fortify products such as cereals, bread, and dairy items.</p><p>In terms of the future outlook, the folic acid market is expected to experience steady growth.
