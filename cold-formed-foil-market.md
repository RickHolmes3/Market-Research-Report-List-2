<p><p>Cold formed foil is a pharmaceutical packaging material that is used for blister packaging of medicines. It is made of aluminum and provides excellent barrier properties to protect the medicines from moisture, air, and light. Cold forming refers to the process in which the aluminum foil is shaped into a cavity by applying pressure at a low temperature. This technique ensures the foil maintains its integrity and provides a tight seal to protect the medicine.</p><p>The future outlook for the cold formed foil market is positive and promising. The pharmaceutical industry is witnessing substantial growth, driven by factors such as a growing aging population, increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, and advancements in healthcare infrastructure.
