<p><p>The laser keyboards market can be divided into two main types: USB and Bluetooth. USB laser keyboards connect to devices through a USB cable, providing a stable and reliable connection. They are commonly used for desktop computers and laptops that have USB ports. Bluetooth laser keyboards, on the other hand, connect wirelessly to devices using Bluetooth technology. They offer more flexibility and mobility as they can be used with any Bluetooth-enabled device, such as smartphones and tablets, without the need for any physical cable connection.</p></p>
