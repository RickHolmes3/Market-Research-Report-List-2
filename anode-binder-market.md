<p><p>The anode binder market can be categorized into two types, water-based and solvent-based. On the other hand, solvent-based types of anode binders utilize solvents as a medium, resulting in improved adhesion and stability. However, these binders may have higher production costs and pose environmental concerns due to the presence of solvents.
