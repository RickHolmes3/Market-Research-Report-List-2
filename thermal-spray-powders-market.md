<p><p>Thermal spray powders are categorized into four main types: ceramics, metals, polymers, and others. Ceramics powders are used to provide thermal insulation and electrical conductivity. Metals powders are employed mainly for corrosion resistance and wear protection applications. Polymer powders, on the other hand, offer advantages such as low friction and high impact resistance. The "others" category encompasses powders made from materials like carbides, cermets, and composites. These powders find various applications in industries including aerospace, automotive, and electronics to enhance surface properties and prolong the lifespan of components.</p></p>
