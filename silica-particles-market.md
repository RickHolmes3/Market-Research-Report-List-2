<p><p>The silica particles market can be categorized into two main types: spherical silica particles and amorphous silica particles. Spherical silica particles are round in shape and have a uniform particle size, which makes them suitable for applications like drug delivery systems, electronic materials, and cosmetics. On the other hand, amorphous silica particles lack a well-defined shape and have a wide range of particle sizes. They are commonly used in industries such as rubber, coatings, and plastics due to their high surface area, absorbent properties, and reinforcing capabilities.</p></p>
