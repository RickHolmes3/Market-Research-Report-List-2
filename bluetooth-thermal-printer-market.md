<p><p>The Bluetooth Thermal Printer market can be classified into two types: Direct Thermal Thermal Printer and Thermal Transfer Thermal Printer. A Direct Thermal Thermal Printer uses heat-sensitive paper to produce images, eliminating the need for ink. It is commonly used for printing receipts and labels. On the other hand, a Thermal Transfer Thermal Printer uses heat to transfer ink from a ribbon onto the paper, making it suitable for printing high-quality and long-lasting images. Both types of printers have Bluetooth connectivity options, allowing wirelessly printing from mobile devices or computers.</p></p>
