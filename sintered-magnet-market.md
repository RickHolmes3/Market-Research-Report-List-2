<p><p>A sintered magnet is a type of permanent magnet made from powdered metals that are compacted and then sintered, or heated to a high temperature without melting, to bond the particles together. Sintered magnets exhibit excellent magnetic properties such as high coercivity and high magnetic remanence, making them ideal for various applications across industries.</p><p>The market analysis of the sintered magnet industry indicates a steady growth trajectory. Factors driving this growth include the increasing demand for efficient and lightweight magnets in industries such as automotive, electronics, and energy. The automotive industry, in particular, is a significant driver of the sintered magnet market, with the growing adoption of electric vehicles and hybrid vehicles. Additionally, the expansion of the wind power industry as a renewable energy source also contributes to the demand for sintered magnets for use in wind turbines.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in magnet technology, such as the development of rare-earth magnets like neodymium-iron-boron and samarium cobalt magnets, have further propelled the growth of the sintered magnet market.
