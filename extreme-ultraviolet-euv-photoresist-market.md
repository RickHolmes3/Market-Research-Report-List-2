<p><p>The Extreme Ultraviolet (EUV) Photoresist market can be categorized into two types: Dry Photoresist and Liquid Photoresist. Dry Photoresist is a type of EUV photoresist that is applied to semiconductor materials in a solid form, and it undergoes a process called sublimation to convert into a vapor for deposition. Liquid Photoresist, on the other hand, is a type of EUV photoresist that is applied to semiconductor materials in a liquid form, and it is typically deposited onto the surface using spin-coating or other similar techniques. Both types of photoresist are critical components in the manufacturing of advanced semiconductor devices.</p></p>
