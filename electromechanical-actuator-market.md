<p><p>The Electromechanical Actuator Market can be categorized into three types based on the motion it produces: Oscillatory Motion, Rotary Motion, and Linear Motion. Oscillatory Motion actuator produces a back-and-forth movement, commonly used in fans or pendulum clocks. Rotary Motion actuator rotates in a circular motion, usually found in motors or wheel mechanisms. Linear Motion actuator moves in a straight line, commonly used in conveyor belts or robotic arms. These types cater to different applications and industries, offering various ways to convert electrical energy into mechanical motion.</p></p>
