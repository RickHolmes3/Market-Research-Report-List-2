<p><p>Industrial drum brakes are commonly used in heavy-duty applications to provide braking functions. There are three main types of industrial drum brakes - simplex, duplex, and servo. </p><p>Simplex drum brakes consist of a single shoe pressing against the drum to generate friction and stop motion. </p><p>Duplex drum brakes employ two shoes pressing against the drum, offering stronger braking power than simplex brakes. </p><p>Servo drum brakes utilize a hydraulic booster or mechanical linkage to increase the force exerted by the shoe, providing even more braking force than duplex brakes. These different types of drum brakes cater to various industrial needs and requirements.</p></p>
