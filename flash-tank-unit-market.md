<p><p>Flash tank units, used in industrial processes, can be classified into two market types: vertical and level. In the vertical market, flash tanks are designed with a vertical orientation, allowing for a compact installation in limited spaces. This type is suitable for applications where space is a constraint. On the other hand, in the level market, flash tanks are designed to be installed at different levels within a process system, accommodating variations in pressure and temperature. This type is ideal for processes that require flexibility in placement and utilization of available space.</p></p>
