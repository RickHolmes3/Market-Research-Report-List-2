<p><p>The automotive wiper components market consists of three main types: wiper blade, wiper motor, and rain sensor. Wiper blades are the rubber or silicone strips attached to the wiper arms that clean the windshield. Wiper motors are the mechanisms responsible for moving the wiper blades. Rain sensors are electronic devices that detect rainfall and automatically activate the wipers. These components work together to ensure clear visibility during rain or snow, enhancing driver safety and comfort while driving.</p></p>
