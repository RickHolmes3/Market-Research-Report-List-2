<p><p>Boat shock absorbers are specialized devices that reduce the impact of waves and bumps on boats, providing a smoother and more comfortable ride. There are two main types of boat shock absorbers: elastomer and plastic. Elastomer shock absorbers are made from rubber-like materials that can deform and absorb impact energy, while plastic shock absorbers are constructed from durable plastic that offers excellent resistance to impact and vibrations. Both types are designed to enhance boat stability, minimize potential damage, and improve overall boating experience.</p></p>
