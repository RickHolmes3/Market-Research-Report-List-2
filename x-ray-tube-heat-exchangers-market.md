<p><p>The X-ray tube heat exchangers market consists of two types: rotating anode type and stationary anode type. In the rotating anode type, the anode rotates at high speeds to dissipate heat generated during X-ray production. This type is commonly used in high-performance systems that require rapid heat dissipation. Meanwhile, the stationary anode type features a fixed anode for heat dissipation, making it suitable for low to mid-performance X-ray systems. Both types cater to different applications and performance requirements within the X-ray tube heat exchangers market.</p></p>
