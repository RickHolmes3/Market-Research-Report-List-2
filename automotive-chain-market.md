<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automotive chain market includes roller chain and silent chain types. Roller chains are made up of interconnected links with roller pins and bushings, widely used for transmitting power in automotive applications. These chains are known for their durability and versatility. On the other hand, silent chains consist of toothed link plates that engage with sprockets, providing accurate positioning with lower noise levels. They are often preferred in engines where reduced noise and vibration are crucial. Both roller and silent chains play key roles in transmitting power and ensuring smooth operation within the automotive industry.</p></p>
