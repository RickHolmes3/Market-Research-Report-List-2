<p><p>Sleeve bearings, also known as bushings, are types of bearings used to reduce friction between rotating shafts and stationary components. There are two main market types: radial sleeve bearing and axial sleeve bearing. Radial sleeve bearings provide support for radial loads, which are perpendicular to the axis of rotation, while axial sleeve bearings handle axial loads, which are parallel to the axis of rotation. Both types are commonly used in various industries and applications to provide smooth and efficient operation while minimizing friction and wear.</p></p>
