<p><p>The medical metal tubing market comprises various types, namely stainless steel, titanium, and nitinol (nickel-titanium or NiTi). These different types of metal tubing play important roles in the medical field, providing reliable and durable solutions for various medical procedures and surgical tools.</p></p>
