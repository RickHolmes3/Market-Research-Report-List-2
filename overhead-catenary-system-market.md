<p><strong>The Overhead Catenary System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flexible Overhead Catenary System</li><li>Rigid Overhead Catenary System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Overhead Catenary System (OCS) market is classified into two main types: Flexible Overhead Catenary System and Rigid Overhead Catenary System. Flexible OCS consists of a flexible wire suspended from a supporting structure, allowing for easier installation and maintenance. It is commonly used in urban areas with curves and bends. On the other hand, Rigid OCS utilizes a rigid structure with reinforced overhead wires, providing higher stability and durability. This type is typically employed in high-speed railways or areas with straight tracks. Both systems play crucial roles in providing electricity to trains and ensuring safe and efficient transportation.</p></p>
