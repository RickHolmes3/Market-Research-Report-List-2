<p><p>A display panel, also known as a display screen or monitor, is an electronic device that displays visual information such as images, videos, texts, or graphics. The increasing adoption of OLED panels in smartphones and televisions is expected to drive market growth.</p><p>Another emerging trend is the integration of touchscreens in display panels. <p><p>The display panel market comprises different types such as LED and OLED. LED panels use light-emitting diodes to emit light. These panels are known for their high brightness, energy efficiency, and longer lifespan. On the other hand, OLED panels use organic compounds that emit light when an electric current is applied. These panels offer better color accuracy, higher contrast ratios, and thinner form factors compared to LED panels. Both LED and OLED panels are widely used in various devices such as televisions, smartphones, and computer monitors for their unique advantages.</p></p>
