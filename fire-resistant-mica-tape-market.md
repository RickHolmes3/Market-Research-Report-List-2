<p><p>Fire resistant mica tape is a type of tape used for insulation in electrical cables and wires. It comes in two main types: Mica Glass Tape and Mica Polyester Tape. Mica Glass Tape is made by laminating mica paper with woven glass fabric, providing high temperature resistance and excellent electrical insulation properties. Mica Polyester Tape, on the other hand, is created by combining mica paper with polyester film, offering a balance of flexibility and fire resistance. Both types of tape contribute to the safety and reliability of electrical systems.</p></p>
