<p><strong>The Automatic Wafer Polisher Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fully Automatic</li><li>Semi-automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic wafer polisher market is divided into two types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic wafer polishers are machines that perform the entire polishing process without any human intervention. They are equipped with advanced technology and have high productivity. On the other hand, semi-automatic wafer polishers require some human intervention for loading/unloading the wafers and adjusting the polishing parameters. These machines offer flexibility and are suitable for smaller-scale production. Both types cater to different market needs and are widely used in the wafer manufacturing industry.</p></p>
