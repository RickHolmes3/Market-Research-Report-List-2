<p><ul><li>Manual Stretch Wrapper</li><li>Semiautomatic Stretch Wrapper</li><li>Automatic Stretch Wrapper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A stretch wrapper is a type of packaging equipment used to wrap pallet loads with a stretch film. There are three main market types of stretch wrappers. Manual stretch wrappers require operators to manually wrap the pallet using a hand-held dispenser. Semiautomatic stretch wrappers use a turntable or rotary arm to automatically wrap the load, but still require manual attachment of the film. Automatic stretch wrappers fully automate the wrapping process, from loading the pallet to attaching and cutting the film. They offer higher efficiency and require minimal operator intervention.</p></p>
