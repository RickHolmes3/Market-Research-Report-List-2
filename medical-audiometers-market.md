<p><p>The medical audiometers market consists of three types of audiometers: standalone audiometer, hybrid audiometer, and PC-based audiometer. </p><p>A Stand-alone audiometer is a self-contained device that can be used independently and does not require additional equipment. It is suitable for use in small clinics or primary care settings.</p><p>A Hybrid audiometer combines the features of a standalone audiometer with the connectivity options of a PC-based audiometer. It can function independently as well as be connected to a computer or software for data management and analysis.</p><p>PC-based audiometers are integrated with a computer or laptop and use specialized software for audiometric testing. They offer advanced functionalities, such as data storage, analysis, and connectivity options, making them suitable for large healthcare facilities or research institutions.</p></p>
