<p><p>Heat Recovery Ventilation (HRV) System is an energy-efficient mechanical system that provides controlled ventilation while recovering heat from indoor air and transferring it to fresh incoming air. It consists of a heat exchanger, fans, filters, and ductwork. HRV systems are designed to remove stale air from a building, while simultaneously introducing fresh air from outside. The recovered heat from the exhaust air is transferred to the incoming fresh air, reducing the need for additional heating or cooling.</p><p>The HRV system market has been witnessing significant growth due to the increasing awareness about indoor air quality, energy-saving technologies, and government regulations regarding energy-efficient buildings. The growing emphasis on reducing carbon emissions and the need for sustainable building solutions are also driving the market growth.</p><p>The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6% during the forecast period.
