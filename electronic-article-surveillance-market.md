<p><p>The Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) market comprises several types of products. Hard tags are physical security devices attached to merchandise, while soft tags are adhesive labels that can be easily removed. Deactivators or detachers are devices used to disable or remove the security tags at the point of sale. Detection systems consist of antennas or sensors that detect the presence of EAS tags on products. Permanent deactivation tags are special tags that are permanently disabled at the point of sale, making them non-reusable.</p></p>
