<p><p>In the semiconductor market, O-rings are utilized in various applications including plasma processes, thermal processes, and wet chemical processes. In plasma processes, O-rings are used to seal the chamber where plasma, gases, and other elements are combined to create a desired effect on the semiconductor material. In thermal processes, O-rings ensure a tight seal in high-temperature environments where heat treatment or annealing of the semiconductor takes place. Lastly, in wet chemical processes, O-rings provide a barrier against corrosive chemicals used in cleaning or etching semiconductors.</p></p>
