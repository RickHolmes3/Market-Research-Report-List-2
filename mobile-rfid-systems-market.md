The LF RFID System operates at low frequencies, typically used in access control applications. The HF RFID System operates at high frequencies, ideal for tracking assets and inventory in industries such as healthcare and retail. The UHF RFID System operates at ultra-high frequencies, suited for long-range applications like supply chain management. The "Others" category includes various RFID technologies like active and passive systems, each with their own unique applications and benefits.</p></p>
