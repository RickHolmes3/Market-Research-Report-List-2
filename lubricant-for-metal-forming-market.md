<p><p>Lubricants for metal forming market comprises various types, such as tube drawing lubricants and hot forming lubricants. Tube drawing lubricants are used in the process of producing tubes by reducing their diameter, ensuring efficient drawing and minimizing friction and wear. On the other hand, hot forming lubricants are specifically designed for high-temperature metal forming operations, providing effective lubrication and protection against heat and oxidation. Both lubricant types play a crucial role in enhancing the metal forming process by reducing friction, extending tool life, and improving product quality.</p></p>
