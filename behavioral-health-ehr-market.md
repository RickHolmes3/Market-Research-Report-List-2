<p><p>The Behavioral Health EHR market can be classified into two types: Cloud-based and On-Premise. Cloud-based EHR systems are hosted on remote servers, allowing access to patient data anytime and anywhere with an internet connection. They offer scalability, lower upfront costs, and automatic updates. On the other hand, On-Premise EHR systems are installed and hosted on local servers within healthcare facilities. They provide greater control and security over data but require higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Healthcare providers select the option based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
