<p><p>The coolant switch valve market offers two types of valves: 2-way and 3-way valves. A 2-way valve has two ports that allow flow in one direction and blocking in the other, controlling the fluid's movement. On the other hand, a 3-way valve has three ports that can direct the flow in either one of the two outlets, or block it altogether. These valves are used in cooling systems to regulate the coolant's flow, ensuring efficient cooling and preventing overheating in various applications.</p></p>
